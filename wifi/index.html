How to Create a WiFi QR Code

Share your WiFi network without dictating long passwords. Enter your network name (SSID), password, and encryption type above, and your QR code is generated instantly, right in your browser.

When guests scan the code with their phone camera, they connect to your WiFi automatically. Works with iPhone (iOS 11+), Android, and all modern devices. No app required.

Perfect for

  • Home networks: stop spelling out passwords for every visitor
  • Airbnb & vacation rentals: print and frame near the router
  • Cafes & restaurants: place on tables or at the counter
  • Offices & coworking spaces: onboard guests instantly

Your data never leaves your browser. No signup, no tracking, no server-side processing.
