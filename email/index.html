How to Create an Email QR Code

Generate a QR code that opens a new email with the recipient, subject, and body pre-filled. Enter the details above and your code is ready instantly.

When someone scans the code, their default email app opens with your message ready to send. No copying addresses, no typos.

Perfect for

  • Business cards & flyers — one scan to reach you by email
  • Feedback forms — place on receipts, packaging, or in-store displays
  • Customer support — print on product manuals or warranty cards
  • Event follow-ups — pre-fill the subject line for easy sorting

Runs entirely in your browser. No data is stored or sent anywhere.
