How to Create a vCard QR Code

Turn your contact details into a scannable QR code. Fill in your name, phone number, email, and any other details above. Your QR code updates in real time.

When someone scans it, your full contact card is added to their phone instantly. No manual typing, no misspelled emails, no missed digits.

Perfect for

  • Business cards: add a QR code so contacts can save your info in one tap
  • Networking events: share details faster than swapping cards
  • Email signatures: link to your vCard QR for easy saving
  • Conference badges & name tags

Everything runs in your browser. Your contact details are never sent to a server.
